Overview

Hairlist is a project created during my studies at Humber College. It is a mobile app designed to help people find skilled hairdressers based on their location and diverse backgrounds for hairstyling purposes.

  • Team
  • Solo
  • Duration
  • 6 weeks
  • Role
  • Branding, User Research, Visual Design, Prototyping

Design Process

Problem Statement

How might we help people find skilled hairdressers that align with their preferences by providing reliable and comprehensive information?

Have you ever had a bad hairstyling experience at a hair salon? It can be challenging to find a good hairdresser, especially after a negative hairstyling experience or when you are newcommer to a particular place. There is no foolproof method to check hair dresserâ€™s skill to ensure a better experience after hairstyling.

Solution

People are uncertain about how to find a skilled hairdresser who can meet their preferences and ensure a positive hairstyling experience. The presence of photos from a hair salon doesn't always guarantee the skills of the hairdresser. What if the hairdresser has left the salon but their photos still exist?

  • Feature 1 : Create an app that provides hairdresser portfolios to help people make informed decisions and trust the skills of the hairdressers.
  • Feature 2 :Allow users to inquire about their hair and book appointments in advance through an app.

- Research

Competitive Analysis

I conducted secondary research and competitive analysis to gain insights into their strengths and weaknesses.

User Interview

I conducted remote user interviews to better understand what factors people consider most important when choosing a hair salon/hairdresser, as well as to learn how to handle situations after experiencing a bad hairstyling outcome.
